# CMC Scholars Flag Raising & Flag Retreat Attendance System

## Overview

The **CMC Scholars Flag Raising & Flag Retreat Attendance System** is a web-based attendance monitoring platform for Carmen Municipal College scholars. This system streamlines and digitizes the process of recording and managing scholar attendance during flag ceremonies and flag retreats, held every Monday to Friday. By replacing traditional paper-based tracking with a digital solution, the system ensures accuracy, transparency, and efficiency in attendance monitoring.

---

## Features

- **Automated Attendance Recording:** Quickly and accurately log scholar attendance for flag raising and retreat ceremonies.
- **Web-Based Interface:** Accessible from any modern web browser, allowing for easy management and monitoring.
- **Paperless Solution:** Eliminates manual paperwork, reducing errors and administrative workload.
- **Secure Scholar Management:** Manage scholar profiles and attendance logs with secure access controls.
- **Reporting & Analytics:** Generate attendance reports to track participation and identify trends.
- **Schedule Management:** Configurable attendance schedules for ceremonies held Monday to Friday.
